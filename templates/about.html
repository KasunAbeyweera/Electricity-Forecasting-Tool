Welcome to our project page, where we are working on developing a forecasting tool to predict electricity demand.

Electricity and power cuts are a common occurrence in many parts of the world, including Sri Lanka, due to a lack of accurate forecasting for energy consumption. This leads to inefficiencies in energy generation, supply, and pricing. To address this issue, we are using advanced AI techniques to develop a powerful machine learning model that can predict electricity consumption and generation, as well as revenue .

Our project focuses on developing an innovative forecasting model that leverages Artificial Neural Networks to analyze a wide range of data inputs such as global fuel prices, weather conditions, expenses in running power stations and the electricity grid, and government policies. The resulting predictions can then be used to optimize energy generation and distribution, as well as to inform decisions on new electricity generation projects.
